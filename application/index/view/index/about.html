What Nutri-Kidz provide.

Nutri-Kidz is a website which aims at helping combat the issues surrounding child obesity in kids aged between 9-12 years of age so that they grow up to be healthy young adults. This website educates children about the importance of having a healthy diet and leading an active life style.
